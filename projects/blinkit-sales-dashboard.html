Power BI Project

Blinkit Grocery Sales Analytics

A Power BI dashboard project focused on analyzing grocery sales, outlet performance, item categories, customer demand patterns, and business KPIs.

Power BI DAX Dashboarding Sales Analysis KPI Design
View GitHub Contact Me

Overview

Business problem

The goal of this project was to understand grocery sales performance through a business dashboard that highlights revenue, outlet contribution, item type performance, and customer-facing product patterns.

Instead of creating only charts, the focus was on designing KPIs that help answer practical business questions.

Process

Analytics workflow

  • Cleaned and structured the dataset for dashboard-ready analysis.
  • Created KPIs for total sales, average sales, item count, and ratings.
  • Analyzed sales by item type, fat content, outlet size, outlet type, and location.
  • Designed slicers and interactive visuals for easy business exploration.
  • Formatted the dashboard to make insights easy to read for recruiters and business users.
